#Exercises {#Exercises}

## SUM OF DIGITS - MIN AND MAX

Write a program that generates 100 random numbers between 2000 and
3000. Among the generated numbers find and display the number with
the largest and smallest sum of digits.


## SYMMETRIC NUMBERS

Write a program that generates 100 random numbers between 1000 and
9999. Among the generated numbers find and display the number, which
are "axially symmetric", which means that their records are of type XYYX
